RTO Root Cause Playbook

📉 Marketing · Diagnosis
Marketing Gone Bad — Structured Triage
3-step diagnostic: (1) Find inflection date; (2) Cross-section by campaigns, audience (gender/age/tier/platform), product; (3) Trace possible changes — spend split, pricing/AOV, listing, assets, audience overlap, domain, EDD, CX. Ends with page quality and relevance analysis.
